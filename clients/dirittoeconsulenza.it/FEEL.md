# diritto&consulenza — what they're going for   (by /feel, 2026-07-02; confidence: full)

## The essence
**diritto&consulenza wants you to feel *held* — surrounded by a whole network of named specialists covering all of Italy, warm as well as expert — so that you can hand over a frightening legal problem without fear.**

The insight: their work is mostly *scary* moments (divorce, custody, medical malpractice, criminal charges, business collapse, debt). Almost every design and copy choice answers that fear with two promises at once — *empatia* and *competenza*.

## What they're trying to achieve
A lead-generation brochure for a multi-city **network** of independent specialist lawyers. The business goal: look bigger and more complete than any solo *studio* while staying personal — "one call reaches the right specialist, anywhere in Italy." Every page funnels to the same conversion — *"Ti possiamo aiutare?"* + a contact form (`persone.md`, `professionisti.md`, every practice area); contact is per-lawyer email, no pricing, no portal. They sell two differentiators: **breadth** (11 practice areas, national coverage, split *persone* vs *imprese*) and **warmth** (empathy-led framing). The audience arrives anxious; the site's job is to make them feel safe enough to reach out.

## What they highlight (ranked — repetition × prominence)
1. **The network-as-honeycomb-covering-Italy** — the single loudest signal. A cluster of colored hexagons in the *outline of Italy* is the homepage hero, given more visual weight than the wordmark itself (`home-fold.png`), then reappears floating in the mission section (`Prearo-mission.png`) and as the backdrop behind the contact form (`professionisti.png`). Intent: *many interlocking specialists, one structure, the whole country.* The org chart *is* the hero image.
2. **Multidisciplinary breadth** — the whole IA forks into *Per le persone* (5 areas) and *Per le imprese* (6 areas), each a hexagon tile (`persone.png`); copy leads with *"Network multidisciplinare"* and *"servizi legali approfonditi e trasversali... una tutela completa"* (`home.md`). They want to feel like a full-service firm assembled from experts.
3. **Named, accountable people** — five lawyers with real **color** headshots, individual profile pages, personal emails, and bylined news articles (`professionisti.png`; `professionisti-avvocato-giovanni-prearo.md`, `...stamerra.md`; `news.md`). Not a faceless brand — real humans you can name and reach.
4. **Empathy + human accompaniment** — the defining line: *"Accompagniamo il Cliente in ogni passo, sostenendolo con **empatia**, rassicurandolo con **competenza** e operando con **esperienza**"* (`home.md`), plus the direct, disarming *"Come possiamo aiutarti?"* / *"Ti possiamo aiutare?"* everywhere, and the serene sky-gradient hero (`cover-prearo.webp`) that is deliberately un-lawyerly.
5. **Rootedness across iconic Italian cities** — Bologna, Lecce and Milano are represented by their most famous *monuments*, not their offices: the medieval Due Torri (`Bologna.webp`), the Baroque Basilica di Santa Croce (`Lecce.webp`), the contemporary CityLife towers at dusk (`TreTorri-Prearo.jpg`). Intent: *real, rooted, national* — and, unspoken, *heritage-to-modern, north-to-south.*

## Tone of voice
Axes: **warm** (not corporate-cold) · **plain & direct** in UI copy but **technically precise** in substance · **modest** (zero bragging — no numbers, awards, or logos) · **reassuring**.

The signature tension is their actual value proposition: *warm on the outside, rigorous on the inside.* The framing is emotional — *"Come possiamo aiutarti?"*, *"Accompagniamo il Cliente... con empatia"* — while the practice copy is exact and unshowy (*"nullità di fideiussioni conformi al modello ABI"*, *"superamento del tasso soglia"*, *"nomina di un Amministratore di Sostegno"*, *"mandataria UIBM, EUIPO, EPO, WIPO"*). They literally spell out the formula: **empatia · competenza · esperienza.**

Nails it:
- *"Accompagniamo il Cliente in ogni passo, sostenendolo con empatia, rassicurandolo con competenza e operando con esperienza."*
- *"Ti possiamo aiutare?"*
- *"Network multidisciplinare."*

## The say–show gap  ← /create's openings
1. **Say "empathy / accompany you every step" — show cool, flat geometry.** The warmth is *asserted* (copy + sky imagery) but the dominant device (hexagons) is abstract and the only genuine human warmth (the headshots) sits *below* the fold. → Bring people and warmth forward; make the honeycomb itself carry humanity instead of holding flat color.
2. **Say "diverse sedi... su tutto il territorio nazionale" — show tourist postcards.** The three cities are famous monuments, not their premises or people at work. Rootedness is symbolic, not documentary. → Either own the place-as-identity idea and execute it beautifully, or ground it in something real.
3. **A strong ownable metaphor, underused in words.** The honeycomb=Italy idea is far stronger visually than the thin verbal *"Network multidisciplinare."* → Make the network/honeycomb concept the narrative spine, not decoration.
4. **Rich, specific practice areas — shown as identical blank cells.** Each area's real substance (sub-services, the named specialist who owns it) is invisible at a glance; every tile is a color + a title + "Scopri di più." → Let each cell reveal its specificity and its human.
5. **An untold story sitting in plain sight.** Medieval Bologna → Baroque Lecce → modern Milan quietly says *tradition meets modernity, north meets south.* They show it but never say it. → Name it; it's a lovely positioning for a national network.

## Signature elements to reinterpret
| their motif | what it means | reinterpretation direction |
|---|---|---|
| Hexagon honeycomb = Italy | the network: interlocking specialists covering the country | Keep the honeycomb as the **spine**, but make cells *hold content* — a practice area, a lawyer's face, a city — and let it assemble into Italy (animated/scroll). A honeycomb sectioning system, not a hero picture. Put faces/landmarks inside cells via **TreatedImage** (duotone). |
| Multicolor cell palette | diversity unified | Make color **semantic**: code *persone* vs *imprese* (and/or each area) so the palette carries meaning, not just decoration. |
| Serene sky-gradient heroes | calm, reassurance | Keep the softness but make it *ownable* — brand-blue/blush **gradient + grain** rather than a generic stock sky. |
| Real team headshots (+ blue caption) | trust lives in named people | **SketchPortrait** or duotone **TreatedImage** of the five lawyers — crafted, warmer than corporate stock, still *them* — and lifted **above the fold**, so the network reads as faces, not just cells. |
| City landmarks (Due Torri / Santa Croce / CityLife) | rooted across Italy; heritage ↔ modern | Reinterpret as *one owned set* — the three monuments in a single duotone/halftone palette, or line-illustrated inside hexagon cells. Turn postcards into a brand language; optionally tell the tradition↔modernity story out loud. |
| The stylized "&" ampersand | law joined to counsel; connection | Elevate the "&" as a hero glyph — oversized in the brand serif, as a **Marquee** or watermark. A ready-made brand mark. |
| *empatia · competenza · esperienza* | their explicit value formula | Make the triad a **structural element** — a three-part **StatBlock**/triptych — so the mission sentence becomes a repeatable brand device, not a one-off line. |

---
**For /create:** lead with the network — but a *warm, human* network. The honeycomb is the gift (own it, fill it with faces and places); the risk to avoid is their current one — a beautiful cool abstraction that keeps the actual humans below the fold. Warmth + rigor, shown together, above the fold.
