# diritto&consulenza — wiki   (crawled 2026-07-02, 12 pages)

## Company overview
**diritto&consulenza** is a multidisciplinary **network of lawyers** ("network di professionisti"), founded (**"Sorto nel"**) **2009**, operating across Italy from three offices: **Bologna, Lecce, Milano**. Positioning line: *"Network multidisciplinare."* Its stated purpose is to guarantee *"servizi legali approfonditi e trasversali"* — deep, cross-cutting legal services giving clients *"una tutela completa."* It serves two audiences it addresses explicitly and separately: **soggetti privati** ("Per le persone") and **imprese** ("Per le imprese"). The site is a client-rendered AngularJS SPA built on the **Avvocato360** platform (theme data-driven; content hydrated from JSON).

## Voice notes
1. **Warm, human, reassuring** — the defining mission line pairs competence with feeling: *"Accompagniamo il Cliente in ogni passo, sostenendolo con **empatia**, rassicurandolo con **competenza** e operando con **esperienza**."*
2. **"Network" is the identity, not "studio"** — repeated framing as *"un network di professionisti"* / *"Network multidisciplinare"*; value = breadth + interlocking specialists ("servizi legali approfonditi e trasversali").
3. **Client-first, plain address** — direct second person: *"Come possiamo aiutarti?"*, *"Ti possiamo aiutare?"*, *"Contatta i professionisti."*
4. **Audience-segmented** — the whole IA forks into *Per le persone* vs *Per le imprese*.
5. **Credible & technical where it counts** — practice copy is specific and jargon-accurate (fideiussioni ABI, tasso soglia, UIBM/EUIPO/EPO/WIPO, negoziazione assistita, amministratore di sostegno). (Deeper analysis is /feel's job.)

## Degradations
- **SPA (AngularJS / Avvocato360):** every URL returns the same 260 KB shell with `{{…}}` bindings and empty `<title>`/`{{pagina.descrizione}}` meta. Per-page HTML distillation impossible; content captured via **WebFetch (rendered)** and marked `source: webfetch` in every page's frontmatter. Only one shell saved to `meta/home.html` (the other 11 routes are byte-identical — not duplicated).
- **Meta descriptions/titles** are client-injected, so per-page `<meta>` was not statically available; page titles reconstructed from rendered content.
- **Images:** WebFetch surfaces few `<img>` (heroes are CSS backgrounds), but the shell embeds the full CDN image list — 65 URLs catalogued, top 19 downloaded by hand (logo/favicon, all 5 lawyers, offices, signature hexagon assets, key stock). `download-images.sh` not used (would have grabbed stock at random up to its 20-cap); `medico-e-paziente.jpg` 404'd on retry and was dropped.
- **Design system:** no published brand guide; DESIGN.md is `source: inferred` from screenshots + embedded theme hex + loaded Google Fonts.
- **Screenshots:** all 7 succeeded via Playwright (JS rendered correctly).
